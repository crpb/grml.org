grml user survey

The first <a href="http://grml.org/user-survey/grml.txt">grml user survey is online</a>. The purpose of this is to help grml developers gain a better idea of what the grml user community is really doing and using, and what aspects people would most like to see improved. Please <a href="http://grml.org/user-survey/grml.txt">contribute</a>!
