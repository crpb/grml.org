grml on FrOSCon 2008

On 23rd and 24th of august some grml developers will visit <a href="http://www.froscon.de/">FrOSCon</a>, a two day conference on Free Software and Open Source, taking place for the third time in Sankt Augustin/Germany. We will have a grml booth as well as a coming-together-hacking-event. Visit us!
