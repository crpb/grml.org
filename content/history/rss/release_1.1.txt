Grml - new stable releases available: grml 1.1, grml64 0.2, grml[64]-medium 0.1

<p>We have new stable releases: grml 1.1 with codename Skunk is available. Take a look at the <a href="http://grml.org/changelogs/README-grml-1.1/">release announcement</a> for details about all the nifty new features. A <a href="http://grml.org/changelogs/README-grml64-0.2/">new stable version of grml64 (0.2)</a> is available as well. And finally we have a new flavour of grml: <a href="http://grml.org/changelogs/README-grml-medium-0.1/">grml-medium 0.1</a> and its 64bit version named <a href="http://grml.org/changelogs/README-grml64-medium-0.1/">grml64-medium 0.1</a>.</p>
