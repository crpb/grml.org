zsh-lovers

zsh-lovers is a small project which tries to collect tips, tricks and examples for the Z shell. The authors of zsh-lovers are me (Michael Prokop) and Christian 'strcat' Schneider. zsh-lovers is part of grml.

If you would like to use zsh-lovers on your debian-system add the following line to /etc/apt/sources.lists (run 'apt-get update && apt-get install zsh-lovers): 

deb http://dufo.tugraz.at/grml/ ./

zsh-lovers is also available as a tar.gz-version: zsh-lovers_0.1.tar.gz (~560kb).
